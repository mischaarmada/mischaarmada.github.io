About

Mischa Armada is a traveler, entrepreneur, and growth advisor from Los Angeles. Previously, she co-founded the venture-backed real estate / alternative accommmodation startup Stayawhile providing members to live in boutique hotels around the world for a flat rate. Her past roles include serving as COO of Imonggo, where she spearheaded their entry into the APAC market, as well driving growth at Honey (Acquired by PayPal), Chideo, and Entertainment Fusion Group where she helped launch Tinder.

A former digital nomad before it became mainstream, she has lived and worked across three continents between L.A., NYC, Manila, Bali and Cape Town.
