Mischa Armada is a multidisciplinary marketing and growth consultant based in Los Angeles. With a focus on multichannel go-to-market strategy, digital strategy, and product marketing, Mischa helps teams identify the most valuable areas to generate growth, discover new business opportunities, and optimize customer experiences that lead to greater operational efficiencies and stronger revenue growth. Her goal is to leverage the learnings she gained from previous experiences of scaling brands, to bring value to the next project.

Mischa combines her passion for creative storytelling, deep understanding of consumer behavior, and ability to translate data into impactful marketing strategies to lead cross-functional teams and execute tactics that incite growth across all consumer touch-points — Increasing awareness, revenue, market share, and mind share.

About Mischa

Mischa Armada is a traveler, entrepreneur, and growth advisor from Los Angeles. A former digital nomad before it became mainstream, she has been working remotely for the last five years, living across three continents between the cities of L.A., NYC, Manila, Bali, and Cape Town.

Previously, she co-founded the venture-backed real estate / alternative accommmodation startup Stayawhile providing members to live in boutique hotels around the world for a flat rate. Her past roles include serving as COO of Imonggo, where she spearheaded their entry into APAC, as well driving growth at Honey (Acquired by PayPal), Chideo, and Entertainment Fusion Group where she helped launch Tinder.

An avid traveler from a young age, Mischa loves exploring the unknown, remote beaches, no itineraries, experiencing new cultures, connecting with fun people, Aperol Spritzes in good company, and hanging out with Butters — her French Bulldog who doubles as both Head of Happiness and an aspiring influencer.
